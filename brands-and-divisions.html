Branches/Brands

By Casper the Ghost
July 5, 2021

As you know, Swivro operates different branches/divisions and brands. Most of our branches are considered a nonprofit, meaning the primary goal is not to make a profit. On this page, you can see all of our public & active branches that we publicly associate ourself aka Swivro with, all privacy-oriented, focused on security. If you have any questions or concerns, do feel free to reach out to us via support@swivro.com

 

1. Speaklink

Speaklink, a privacy-first communication platform. End-to-end encryption for messaging, calling, video calling, and screen sharing. We value your privacy.

Visit Speaklink

 

2. GitPear

GitPear.com, an alternative to GitHub offering free access to create public and private repositories to store, maintain, and update your code for your personal or business project.

Visit GitPear

 

3. Levin Passwords

Remember one master password to access all of your passwords, anywhere, on any device. No sync needed. When the same values are entered for all the fields below, the generated password will always result the same.

Visit Levin

 

4. AtlasDNS

AtlasDNS is a libre (free) privacy-oriented secure DNS Hosting platform. AtlasDNS allows you to manage your domain name’s DNS Records quick and seamlessly. UNLIMITED DNS Records, and up to 50 domain names, for free, with no payment info required.

Visit AtlasDNS
