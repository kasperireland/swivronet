How is "unlimited disk space/storage" possible?



To be clear, we do not refer to it as "unlimited disk space", but instead, "unmetered". This means there is initially no set limit to the amount of disk space you can store on your web hosting account. Keep in mind, Terms & Service + Fair Use Policy Apply. If you exceed 25GB, you will receive an email questioning why you are using this amount of disk space (you must respond within 48 hours), and if it seems unreasonable, we will limit your Inodes (Files + Folders Total Amount) to 350,000.
