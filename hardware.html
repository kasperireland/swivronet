All of our cPanel servers run on Pure-SSD Drives, whilst our DirectAdmin servers run on NVME Drives. There's really no noticeable difference at all, but when using a disk speed test tool, NVME does win usually. NVME Drives will not have a major positive impact on your website over SSD's.

We use both the cPanel Software and the DirectAdmin Software to provide web hosting to our customers. cPanel has been the worlds industry leading web hosting control panel for decades, whilst DirectAdmin is just now getting more recognition and users. Some may say DirectAdmin is better than cPanel, but we think they are different and are good in their own ways.

Our cPanel Server features the Intel Xeon Processor (Skylake, IBRS), while our DirectAdmin servers also feature either an Intel Xeon Processor or a Ryzen 3900X depending on the Server.

Our servers, whether running DirectAdmin or cPanel, run CloudLinux OS, AlmaLinux OS, or Debian, depending on the server location, size, and purpose. All of our DirectAdmin servers are being migrated to AlmaLinux using custom code to limit resources per-user rather than using CloudLinux for that.
