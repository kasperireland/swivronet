    cPanel is used for all hosting account provisioning.
  • How many websites can I host?
    Depending on the plan you ordered, the amount of websites you can host is unmetered. There is no initial set limit.

Timeline of Swivro History

September 9, 2016

DiskNet is founded

An unregistered nonprofit organization called DiskNet is founded, with the primary focus of developing open source software for Windows, Web, and Linux. DiskNet started with a budget of $0

February 17, 2019

Primary Focus shifts to Web Hosting

Whilst we offered web hosting from the day we were founded, it wasn't our primary focus. As our web hosting service grew while our open source software did not, we decided to shift our primary focus to web hosting under the domain LitePanel.net. Our primary focus will not permanently be Web Hosting.

September 25, 2019

Swivro arrives

On September 25th, 2019, the domain name swivro.com was registered for the first time. We then began operating under the name Swivro. We're still considered a nonprofit organization, and all "payments" are considered donations and do not constitute a customer transaction. The name Swivro doesn't mean anything, aka its not in the dictionary, but Swivro defines Swivro.
