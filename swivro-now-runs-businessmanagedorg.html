Swivro now runs BusinessManaged.org

By Dmitri
July 15, 2021

Hello friends,

On July 13th, 2021, Swivro acquired & took over BusinessManaged.org

BusinessManaged.org is a managed web hosting platform. BusinessManaged.org will build, manage, maintain, and host your own website for as low as $2 per month (if paid yearly at $24/year). BusinessManaged.org also provides web hosting for people on the pro side of websites & all things web hosting, with an advanced web hosting control panel (DirectAdmin Control Panel). BusinessManaged.org is basically a great option for people who want to build a website, know what content they want their website to have, but need some help getting there. Rather than figuring all of this out on your own, and spending your own time, BusinessManaged.org can do it all for you.

On July 13th, 2021, when we acquired BusinessManaged, we got a new domain name for BusinessManaged (being businessmanaged.org) and redesigned the entire website (it had no official website previously). Whether you are trying to create a business or personal website, BusinessManaged.org can work out great for you. If you are in need of a custom solution, and our standard web hosting isn't powerful enough, simply open a live chat session with us and we can give you a quote.

Although BusinessManaged is indeed very affordable, with all products at a very low price, BusinessManaged now only offers support (via live chat) every day of the week & weekend, 1:30PM-5:30PM PT/PST, or 4:30PM-8:30PM ET/EST. After the acquisition of BusinessManaged, we began migrating cloud instance/virtual server service providers from Amazon Web Services (AWS) to BuyVM by FranTech. This allows for increased stock (aka how many times x product can be ordered before running out of space/stock), less limitations, and faster speeds. This is definitely a service quality increase for BusinessManaged.org

We've also finalized a new pricing structure for BusinessManaged.org which is now live. Existing clients have been swapped to the new pricing structure. No existing clients are unhappy, because the new pricing structure is even more affordable (est 75% decrease in price). BusinessManaged.org's Enterprise Managed plan/option was previously (rounded up) $350 per year, and is now $195 per year. BusinessManaged will continue to use Fastspring as a payment processor aka billing solution and Tawk for a live support chat.

BusinessManaged.org is NOT one of our libre/free services and is our ONLY service which REQUIRES a payment to be made before being able to access the service.

See the NEW & IMPROVED BusinessManaged.org at https://businessmanaged.org

Thanks for reading and have a nice day.
